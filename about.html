I get a small credit commission (300 credits) which I will send immediately back to you, if you give me your DJ profile name using the contact page here. Guides & tools

I put together some guides on how I approach bot-making over on the DreamJourney server. I’ve now started to consolidate them here for ease of access and readability! Just things like character building, how to wrangle lorebooks, and getting the most out of DreamJourney. They’re just how I do things, not rules! Making bots isn’t as hard as it seems, or I wouldn’t be doing it 🤡

I also made Aster, a browser extension for DreamJourney with a bunch of quality-of-life stuff: chat counter, saving regens, input recovery, new tools for creators to test their lorebooks, and more. It started small and kind of got out of hand (in a fun way).
