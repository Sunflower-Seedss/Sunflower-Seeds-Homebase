Northbound

Post-Apocalyptic · Survival · Horror
✦ Play: Northbound Survival Scenario →
anypov dark gritty mutant postapocalyptic dystopian zombie

𝔸ℕ𝕐ℙ𝕆𝕍 ◇ 𝕊𝔽𝕎 𝕀ℕ𝕋ℝ𝕆

Society is dead, rotting and mutating under the weight of a viral fungus. Survival means heading north before winter, or so it’s thought. Found by a small group of survivors, it’s adapt or die. You can be anyone, or anything. Injured and in need of help, an absolute ass ready to take everything they have, leader of your own group of survivors, or someone who’s secretly being mutated by the virus-fungus combo that’s ravaged the world.

tw: body horror, gore, death, mutant monster things and very bad people

Set-up

In a world ravaged by the mutated CordyV-13 virus and the fungus that follows in its wake, survival is a brutal, unforgiving struggle. A small group of survivors battle not only the infected, but also the harsh elements, dwindling resources, and the ever-present tension between them. Trust is fragile, alliances shift, and every choice has consequences. As they move northward through a dangerous, crumbling world, they encounter more than just monsters,they face the darkness within humanity itself. In this unforgiving new reality, no one is safe.

Modern day · currently travelling through Minnesota · aiming to get as far north as possible, hopefully into Canada · 8 months since everything went to shit

Scenes

Default Scenario

The group is currently split, with Cole, Amara and Eli scavenging in an old house while Nadia, Marcus, Tess and Travis are at their makeshift camp in another house about 20 minutes away, going over provisions. They hear a noise coming from the second floor (that’s you!), and carefully move to investigate, ready to face any threat.

Trapped

Uh-oh! Silly you got caught in one of Travis’ snares and spent the night dangling from a tree. I hope you’re really convincing, otherwise when he finds you, he might leave you there.

Injured

The group encounters you being chased by the infected. They scare off the monsters, but you’re injured. Will they help you, leave you be, or put you out of your suffering?

Storefront Standoff

The group is picking through an old gas station store, when you, or your group, get the jump on them first. I’ve only described the barrel of a gun entering the scene, so it can be yours or maybe a member of your own travelling group. A good start if you wanna play someone who’s a little more of a badass.

Massacre

The group finds you, covered in blood and surrounded by newly deceased. But it doesn’t add up. Why are you alive? Why didn’t the infected kill you, too? Kind of setting it up for you to have some kind of power or maybe a mutation yourself! But you could also just be lucky. As always, up to you~
